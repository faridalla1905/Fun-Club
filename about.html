About

Built by students, for students.

Fun Club began as a student-led initiative to support learning, leadership, and service in a welcoming community environment.

Story

Fun Club is a student-led educational organization founded in 2020 to help children strengthen their English language skills while building confidence, creativity, and leadership.

Through weekly learning sessions, interactive games, educational activities, STEM projects, and community service, Fun Club creates a supportive environment where students can learn, grow, and give back to their communities.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To help students build confidence, academic skills, leadership, and a service mindset through supportive learning experiences.

Vision: A community where every student has access to encouragement, mentorship, and opportunities for growth.
