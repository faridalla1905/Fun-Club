Volunteer

Join the work behind the impact.

Fun Club volunteers help with tutoring, mentoring, activities, events, college readiness, and student leadership projects.

Requirements

Volunteers should be reliable, respectful, patient, and willing to support students in a positive environment.

Benefits

Volunteers gain service hours, leadership experience, mentoring practice, teamwork skills, and real community impact.

FAQ

Common questions.

Can students volunteer?

Yes. Student volunteers can support sessions, projects, activities, and events.

How often are sessions?

Fun Club is structured around weekly Saturday learning sessions.

What do volunteers do?

Tutoring, mentoring, organizing, STEM help, college readiness support, and event assistance.
