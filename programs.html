Programs

Practical support for student growth.

Each program is designed to help students gain skills they can actually use in school, college, careers, and life.

English Learning

Students practice speaking, reading, writing, vocabulary, and cultural understanding in a low-pressure environment.

College Readiness

Students receive support with goal-setting, college awareness, resumes, career exploration, and planning for the future.

Leadership

Students lead projects, communicate with peers, plan activities, and build teamwork and responsibility.

STEM Activities

Students explore technology, coding, design, problem-solving, and hands-on creative projects.

Fun Club helps students learn, lead, and serve through education, mentoring, and volunteer opportunities.
